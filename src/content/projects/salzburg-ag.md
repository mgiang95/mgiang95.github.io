## Problem

Domestic solar systems produce data that homeowners rarely understand — and
the app meant to make energy tangible was being built from scratch, with no
design foundation to keep a growing feature set consistent.

## Approach

I built the design system and designed the UI for
[AVA](https://presse.salzburg-ag.at/news-ava-app-die-persoenliche-energie-steuerung-fuer-daheim?id=177009&menueid=30119&l=deutsch),
Salzburg AG's mobile app for managing home solar systems — tokens,
components and patterns first, screens second. In parallel I designed the
user research methodology for [enox.energy](https://www.enox.energy/de-at),
an IoT platform for decentralized energy systems.

## Outcome

AVA launched from zero and reached over 1,500 users in the initial rollout;
the enox research led to concrete accessibility improvements. My first
end-to-end run of the pattern I still work by: system before screens.
